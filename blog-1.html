Origin Story

What is Roast&Rich? Why we built this — and why you need it

📅 2026 ⏱ 4 min read 🎯 All Gen Z

Let's be honest. Most finance apps are boring as hell.

They give you pie charts. They say things like "optimize your portfolio allocation." They assume you already know what a "portfolio" is. They're basically built for 40-year-old finance bros — not for 19-year-old college students in Jaipur trying to figure out why there's ₹0 left before the 25th of the month.

💡 That's the gap we wanted to fill. A finance tool that actually speaks Gen Z — with Hinglish, with sass, with real numbers, and zero condescension.

The idea: what if finance felt like a roast?

You know how roasts are actually super effective? Like when your best friend says, ‘You order ₹2000 worth of biryani and then complain about being broke?’ — it stings, but it also makes you think.

That's the core idea behind Roast&Rich. What if instead of dry advice, your finance app actually called you out? Not to shame you — but to show you the mirror, in the most Gen Z way possible.

We wanted an app that says:

  • "₹3,200 on food delivery this month. That’s literally a month’s SIP, gone on cravings."
  • "This ₹4,500 jacket eats 40% of your remaining budget. Wait a week."
  • "Zero investments for 3 months running. Compounding is crying."

And then also — fixes it. Actually gives you a plan.

Who is Roast&Rich actually for?

Everyone from age 13 to 28. We cover all of Gen Z:

  • 🎒 School students — managing pocket money, building first habits
  • 🎓 College students — hostel budgets, Food-app addiction, first investments
  • 💼 First jobbers — first salary, EMI decisions, "should I buy this phone?"
  • 💻 Freelancers — irregular income, tax nightmares, no boss to help
🚨 Important: Most finance tools are built for people who already have money and know what to do with it. We're built for people who are figuring it all out in real time.

What does the app actually do?

We built 6 core tools, all in one place:

  • 🔥 Money Roast — enter your monthly spend, get brutal honesty + a fix plan
  • 🚦 FOMO Filter — about to impulse buy? Check if it's actually worth it first
  • 📈 Wealth Builder — see your money compounding live, from today to retirement
  • 📊 Smart Budget — AI generates a budget personalised for YOUR city and situation
  • 🎯 Goal Tracker — car, house, trip — every dream has a monthly savings target
  • 💬 AI Chat — ask anything, AI already knows your full profile

Why now? Why us?

Because the financial literacy gap in Gen Z is massive. We weren't taught this in school. Our parents' advice ("save karo beta") is too vague. And most online content is either too technical or trying to sell you something.

Roast&Rich is not here to sell you anything. No ads. No commissions. Just a tool that's genuinely on your side.

🚀 Bottom line: We built the app we wish existed when we were 18, broke, and completely confused about money. If that sounds like you — you're in the right place.

Now go get roasted. 💀
