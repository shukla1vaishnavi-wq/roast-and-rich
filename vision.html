Our Vision

Roast&Rich

Where brutal honesty meets financial freedom — for every Gen Z in India.

The Big Picture

We envision a world where every young person in India — regardless of background, college, or income — has access to financial education that actually makes sense. Not textbook jargon. Not bank seminars designed for 45-year-olds. Real, honest, actionable guidance that speaks their language and respects their intelligence.

Roast&Rich exists to close the gap between what schools teach (nothing about money) and what life demands. We want to be the financial wake-up call that every 16–28 year old needs — delivered with honesty, humour, and genuine care.

"A country's financial future depends on whether its young people learn money skills at 20 or regret not learning them at 40. We choose 20."

What We're Building Toward

Five years from now, we want Roast&Rich to be the first financial tool a college student opens when they get their first salary. A platform that follows them from their hostel days to their first home — adapting with them as their financial life evolves.

We're building toward a future where:

Financial Safety
Every Gen Z has an emergency fund before lifestyle inflation hits
Early Investing
SIPs started at 20, not 35 — because time is the real investment
Smart Decisions
Spending, saving, and investing decisions made with full information

Who This Is For

We built Roast&Rich for 13-to-28-year-olds — school students managing pocket money, college students surviving hostels, interns earning their first stipend, and first-jobbers figuring out adulting. For anyone who ever thought "I should really figure out this money thing" and then didn't, because no one made it approachable.

This is the financial co-pilot we all wish we had at 18.
